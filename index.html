Privacy Policy for Arizona RFP Proposal Writer GPT

Last Updated: May 7, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Arizona RFP Proposal Writer GPT ("we", "our", or "the Service") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Custom GPT.

Information Collection

The Arizona RFP Proposal Writer GPT integrates with a search API hosted at https://9bb72b27-1298-4fb8-b7a0-56821adcda92-00-3ma9v6gsnied5.riker.replit.dev that enables searching of Medicaid RFP documents. When you use this GPT:

  1. Your search queries are processed by our API to retrieve relevant document information
  2. We do not store or retain your conversation history or search queries
  3. No personal identifying information is collected or stored

How We Use Information

The information processed through our API is used solely for:

  • Providing relevant document search results in response to your queries
  • Improving the search functionality and accuracy

Third-Party Services

This GPT uses:

  • OpenAI's ChatGPT platform to process conversations
  • Pinecone vector database to store and search document embeddings
  • OpenAI's embedding API to convert text into vector embeddings

These services are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access or disclosure of data processed through our API.
